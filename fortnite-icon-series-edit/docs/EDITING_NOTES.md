## üé¨ Transition Styles

### Slow Section (Early creators)
- **Cross Dissolve** (0.5-1 second)
- **Fade to white** between eras
- **Smooth zoom** transitions

### Medium Section (Middle creators)
- **Quick cuts** on beat
- **Whip pan** transitions
- **Match cuts** (similar poses/actions)

### Fast Section (Modern creators)
- **Hard cuts** on every beat
- **Flash frames** (1-frame white flash)
- **Glitch effects** for intensity

### The Drop
- **Bass-synchronized cuts**
- **Screen shake** on drops
- **Zoom bursts** on key moments

## üé® Effects & Filters

### Recommended Effects
1. **Color Correction** - Match tones across different sources
2. **Sharpening** - Enhance clarity for game footage
3. **Vignette** - Subtle darkening around edges for focus
4. **Motion Blur** - Add on fast movements for fluidity
5. **Speed Ramps** - Slow-mo into fast-forward for impact

### Effects to Avoid
- ‚ùå Over-saturation (too cartoony)
- ‚ùå Heavy film grain (looks dated)
- ‚ùå Excessive lens flares (distracting)
- ‚ùå Comic-style effects (doesn't match tone)
- ‚ùå Random screen distortion (unless intentional for drop)

## üîä Audio Mixing

### Music Layers
- **Main track:** "Last of My Kind" full mix
- **Optional:** Stem separation for creative control
  - Vocals only for certain moments
  - Instrumental only for real-life clips
  - Drums isolated for intensity

### Sound Effects
- **Whoosh sounds** on transitions (subtle)
- **Impact sounds** on beat drops
- **Creator voice clips** (reactions, catchphrases)
- **Game audio** (pickaxe swings, glider sounds) mixed low

### Audio Levels
- Music: -3dB to -6dB (allow headroom)
- Sound FX: -12dB to -15dB (subtle enhancement)
- Voice clips: -6dB to -9dB (clear but not overpowering)
- The Drop: 0dB (full volume for impact)
